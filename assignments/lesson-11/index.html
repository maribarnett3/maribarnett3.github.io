Events

Preston is the perfect place for outdoor activities. The summers are warm and beautiful. It is surrounded by mountains and lovely lakes with plenty of opportunities for recreational activities and fun. The winter brings the brightest snow and the most striking scenery. Activities such as snowmobiling and cross country skiing are popular. Travelling down Mink Creek, Cub River, and even Logan Canyon, we can see the most stunning winter wonderland ever, ending up at Bloomington or Bear Lake. Spring also has some fun activities such as golfing at the 18-hole course, floating and kayaking down the Bear River, and fishing. What’s in the Sky, brings new stories and information about city events weekly, focusing on the weather forecasting.
